## Environmental Control

Biopharmaceutical manufacturing facilities must be designed to maintain strict environmental control. This includes:

- **Cleanrooms:** Specific ISO classifications (e.g., ISO 5, ISO 7, ISO 8).
- **HVAC Systems:** Specialized systems for air filtration (HEPA) and pressure cascades.
- **Unidirectional Flow:** Strict routing of personnel and materials to prevent cross-contamination.

### Key Considerations
* Material of construction (smooth, cleanable surfaces)
* Utility systems (WFI, clean steam, process gases)
* Waste management and bio-decontamination
