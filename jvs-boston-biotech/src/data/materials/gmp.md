## What is GMP?

Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) is a system for ensuring that products are consistently produced and controlled according to quality standards. It is designed to minimize the risks involved in any pharmaceutical production that cannot be eliminated through testing the final product.

### Core Principles
1. **Written Procedures:** Everything must be documented.
2. **Training:** Personnel must be properly trained.
3. **Validation:** Processes and equipment must be validated.
4. **Traceability:** Full history of every batch must be available.
